About Us

Namaste!

India Association (IA) is one of the most active Recognized Student Organizations (RSO) at Missouri S & T. Our mission is primarily focused on helping the Indian student community while nurturing the culture & traditions of India. We have always believed in cherishing our diverse Indian roots & culture. At IA, Indian festivals are more than just events. They are an expression of our Indian tradition and diversity. We help new students gel in with our closely knit community, and we welcome you to join our extended family halfway around the world from home.

India Association helps all incoming students with an airport pick up service along with temporary accommodation, until they find a permanent place to stay. IA also provides pick-up and drop services to Lambert International Airport at St. Louis during the Thanksgiving, spring, winter & summer holiday breaks.

IAâ€™s finest contribution to the Missouri S & T community takes place twice every academic year for conducting an event for Diwali & Holi. These are some of the biggest shows that take place on campus each year, where IA members display an amazing array of talent in different art performances & culinary skills! (Yes, students cook dinner for more than 800+ people who attend these events.)

Check back on our website for regular updates about our events & other India related news!

Fall 15 Pickups & Drops

Fall is about to fall onto us (pun intended!) and India Association is pleased to provide our annual pick up & drop services from Rolla to St.Louis (or the other way) for all current & incoming students starting from the 25th of July 2015.

Find the Google Form link at the end of this post, that you need to fill up in order to have a pick-up or drop arranged for you. Please do make sure that you've read all the instructions on the Google Form before filling it up. We recommend that you re-confirm all the details that you have entered in the form before submitting it since any errors will hinder our ability to provide you with our services. In case your travel plans do change, we request you to email us at india@mst.edu atleast 48 working hours (US Central Time) earlier, so that we can make alternate arrangements.

Please note that the pickups & drops is a voluntary service by India Association at Missouri S&T. Although we try to help you personally to the best of our abilities, we cannot legally guarantee you any support or assistance.

Once we have received your information, you will receive an email from us confirming the ride with other details.
