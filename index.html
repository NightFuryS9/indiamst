About Us

Namaste!

India Association (IA) is one of the most active Recognized Student Organizations (RSO) at Missouri S & T. Our mission is primarily focused on helping the Indian student community while nurturing the culture & traditions of India. We have always believed in cherishing our diverse Indian roots & culture. At IA, Indian festivals are more than just events. They are an expression of our Indian tradition and diversity. We help new students gel in with our closely knit community, and we welcome you to join our extended family halfway around the world from home.

India Association helps all incoming students with an airport pick up service along with temporary accommodation, until they find a permanent place to stay. IA also provides pick-up and drop services to Lambert International Airport at St. Louis during the Thanksgiving, spring, winter & summer holiday breaks.

IA’s finest contribution to the Missouri S & T community takes place twice every academic year for conducting an event for Diwali & Holi. These are some of the biggest shows that take place on campus each year, where IA members display an amazing array of talent in different art performances & culinary skills! (Yes, students cook dinner for more than 800+ people who attend these events.)

Check back on our website for regular updates about our events & other India related news!

Colour Splash

Holi Time!

A festival that celebrates the victory of good over evil and a celebration of the arrival of spring and harvests to come. It’s the festival of colors, emotions, and happiness. And what better way to express yourself than with the vibrant colors of the rainbow?

Come, join us at Schumman Park on the 10th of April from 10 AM and get doused with color, fun & habbiness! Bring everyone you want to celebrate this joyous festive occasion with India Association! Now you may ask us, what it costs to have this much fun, and we say to you, your awesome presence is all that’s required! It’s free to attend!

Do spread the word. Feel free to share the event details to the campus community and groups. With open arms we embrace to make it as diverse and multicultural as possible.

Date: Sunday April 10, 2016

Time: 10 am to Noon

Venue: Schuman Park - 404 E 16th St, Rolla, MO 65401

Holi Cultural Show

If it ain’t good enough that you get a literal blast of color during our Holi Splash on the 10th of April, make sure you can be a part of some more Holi festivities by making it to the Holi Cultural Show on the 17th of April at 4pm that will take you away to a world of Indian dance, music and awesomeness! And once your minds have been filled with the colors of India, make way for some luscious, mouthwatering, delectable and running-out-of-words-to-describe-awesome-Indian-food dinner at Havener Center!

India Association will be selling tickets for the show and dinner outside of the Curtis Laws Wilson Library and at Havener Center from 11.30AM to 1.30PM throughout this week and the next! If you have any queries, don’t hesitate to contact us on india@mst.edu !

Date: Sunday April 17, 2016

Cultural Show begins at 4pm followed by Dinner at 6 pm

Venue: St. Pat's Ballroom, Havener Center

Roommate Requirement

Fill up the Roommate Requirement Google Form at the end of this post with your relevant details as required. Be sure to state your preferences such as your love for Valyrian steel, Dragon heartstring, Jimi Hendrix or Mughlai chicken, so that you’d be able to find that perfect roommate(s)!
